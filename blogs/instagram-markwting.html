5.Posting Tips

* Interact with other business profiles
* Engage with other pages and creators
* Use all the features like post content, use stories, do video, etc.
* Ask questions and do shout out
* Tagging especially in stories as they can then share on their profiles new
* Regular and consistent content that brings a value
* Use all 30 hashtags. Don’t use always same hashtags
* Respond comment who comment on your post
* Go back and like the old posts and then post a new post, that will bring people back to see new post
